Pizza Recipe

Description:

A timeless Italian classic, the Margherita pizza is the gold standard of sourdough or yeast-based pies. It features a thin, crispy crust topped with a simple yet vibrant tomato sauce, creamy fresh mozzarella, and aromatic basil leaves.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 245°C.
  2. Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Arrange the sliced mozzarella on top of the sauce.
  5. Top with fresh basil leaves.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
